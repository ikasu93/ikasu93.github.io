About me

Hello, my name is Dinh-An Ho and I am 27 years old. I am currently living in Germany and I graduated with my master's degree in Computer Science at the RWTH Aachen University.

I was born in Germany (with Vietnamese roots) and I have always been enthusiastic about mathematical tasks and challenges. That is one reason why I decided to study Computer Science. Furtheremore, I am interested in the technological progress and would like to contribute to the development of digitalization in whatever form (be it as a consultant, as a developer or as an analyst). For me, technological progress is a challenge and an opportunity for a richer life. To make this possible, I want to contribute with my technical skills, but also with my personal skills that I have acquired over the years. My goal is to gain as much experience as possible, to adapt and to help with my knowledge.

Besides programming, my passion is painting. I like to spend my free time with my family and friends, who inspire and motivate me with their passions. It is hard to name all of them. Anyway, these are some of my favorite interests:
