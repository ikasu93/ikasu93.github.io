About me

Hello, my name is Dinh-An Ho and I am 28 years old. I am currently living in Germany and have graduated with my master's degree in Computer Science at the RWTH Aachen University in December 2020.

I was born in Germany (with Vietnamese roots) and I have always been enthusiastic about mathematical and technical challenges. That is one reason why I decided to study Computer Science. Furthermore, I am quite interested in technologies, such as Artificial Intelligence & Bitcoin(Lightning Network). For me, technological progress is a challenge and an opportunity for a richer life. Therefore, I currently work as an IT Business Analyst for one of the largest IT service providers in the world, where I help specify requirements for software for a large logistics company.

Besides programming, my passion is painting. I like to spend my free time with my family and friends, who inspire and motivate me with their passions. It is hard to name all of them. Anyway, these are some of my favorite interests:
