About me

Hey there, I'm Dinh-An Ho, a passionate adventurer navigating the exciting world of technology. I proudly earned my master's degree in Computer Science from the RWTH Aachen University, fueling my interest for mathematical and technical challenges.

Born in Germany with Vietnamese roots, I embody a blend of cultural influences that shape my unique perspective. It's this diverse tapestry that propelled me towards the fascinating realm of Computer Science. Always on the lookout for the next breakthrough, I find myself captivated by cutting-edge technologies such as Artificial Intelligence. To me, technological progress isn't just a means to an end; it's an invitation to a more enriching existence.

Currently, I thrive as an IT Business Analyst at one of the world's largest IT service providers, where I unlock the potential of software by translating complex requirements for a major logistics company. Collaborating with brilliant minds in the industry fuels my passion for problem-solving and empowers me to make a tangible impact.
