About me

Hello, my name is Dinh-An Ho and I am 27 years old. I am currently living in Germany and I am studying Computer Science in the Master's program at the RWTH Aachen University.

I was born in Germany (with Vietnamese roots) and I have always been enthusiastic about mathematical tasks and challenges. That is why I decided to study Computer Science. I am also interested in the technological progress of our time and would like to contribute to the development of digitalization in whatever form (be it as a consultant, as a developer or as an analyst). My goal is to gain as much experience as possible, to adapt and to help with my knowledge.

Besides programming, my passion is painting. I like to spend my free time with my family and friends, who inspire and motivate me with their passions. It is hard to name all of them. Anyway, these are some of my favorite interests:



Computer Science

    I enjoy working on software. It motivates me to find new approaches to different problems.

    "Everyone should learn how to code, it teaches you how to think." - Steve Jobs

Sport

    Whether it's soccer or volleyball, it is always a breathtaking feeling to win together with the team on the field. Everyone has his role, but only as a whole it is possible to win.

    "We are team. One person struggles, we all struggle, one person triumphs, we all triumphs." - Lionel Messi

Art

    I like to be inspired by the world. Painting gives me the freedom to develop myself creatively.

    "All you need to paint is a few tools, a little instruction, and a vision in your mind" - Bob Ross

Travelling

    Travelling means enriching your own life. It means to broaden your own horizon by discovering something beyond your everyday life.
